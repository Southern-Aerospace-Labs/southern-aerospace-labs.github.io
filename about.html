About

Based in Melbourne, Australia, Southern Aerospace Labs (SAL) was founded to drive research and development into the space industry in the Southern Hemisphere.

The Australian Space Agency was first established in the last five years, and there has been a broad acceleration of the efforts in the Southern Hemisphere to contribute to space missions.

As the world prepares for a new space revolution with the formation of permanent human bases beyond Earth, such as the Moon and Mars, it is important that the Southern Hemisphere is at the forefront.

Our Principles

Vision

We envision a Solar System where all humans can participate in and benefit from space. We believe this future depends on space systems that are intuitive, accessible, and designed around human needs â€” not just for specialists, but for anyone building, operating, or relying on space technology. From the southern hemisphere, we are building the foundations for a more open, inclusive, and sustainable space economy.

Values

We strive for an environment that is led by processes that encourage rapid iteration, refinement, experimentation and lots of failures. By creating a system in which individuals can prototype safely and craft new software and hardware with passion and care, we want to continue to strive towards our vision. Paired with thoughtful, human-centered design, we want to continue to ensure that the space ecosystem is accessible for all.

Mission

Our mission is to design and build intuitive, reliable software and hardware that enable humans to explore and operate in space. By lowering barriers to participation in space technology, we aim to support a more inclusive, open, and sustainable space economy. We want to unlock new opportunities in the space economy. To achieve this, we want to make Australia the central hub for space-related activities in the Southern Hemisphere.
